Keith Weber

Empowered by my dream career devoted to researching and implementing public policy within urban environments. I hope to bring change to the enduring problems associated with structural racism in America. In my undergraduate career, I have served as chair for an international research team abroad, deployed a philanthropy for wounded troops, chartered a non-profit organization to generate revenue for addiction relief grants (see Professional Development tab) and currently serve on the Special Alcohol Fund Advisory Committee for the city of Manhattan. To help better convey my experiences, I taught myself to code and created this website. I will graduate from Kansas State University in Spring of 2018 with degrees in Psychology and Anthropology, and a minor in Non-Profit Leadership Studies.
