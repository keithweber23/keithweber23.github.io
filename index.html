Keith Weber

I am empowered by my dream career devoted to researching and implementing public policy within urban environments. I hope to bring change to the enduring problems associated with structural racism in America. In my undergraduate career, I have served as chair for an international research team abroad, deployed a philanthropy for wounded troops, chartered a non-profit organization to generate revenue for addiction relief grants (see Professional Development tab), and currently serve as Co-Chair on the Special Alcohol Fund Advisory Committee for the city of Manhattan. To aid me in articulating my experiences, I taught myself to code and created this website. I will graduate from Kansas State University in Spring of 2018 with degrees in Psychology and Anthropology, and a minor in Non-Profit Leadership Studies.
